Place your PEM certificates here:

  config/tsa-cert.pem   — TSA signing certificate (with timeStamping EKU)
  config/tsa-key.pem    — TSA private key

See CERTIFICATE-SETUP.md for detailed instructions on issuing these
from the smallstep CA at 192.168.1.2.

IMPORTANT: Keep tsa-key.pem private.
           chmod 600 config/tsa-key.pem
